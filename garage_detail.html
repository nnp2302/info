Garage Management Website

Technogy used: PHP HTML5 JavaScript CSS3 Ajax MySql Bootstrap MVC

A website dedicated to car garage management. This platform serves as a comprehensive solution for vehicle owners, offering functionalities to schedule maintenance appointments and providing a user-friendly interface for garage management for corporate members. Whether it's scheduling routine maintenance or efficiently managing garage operations within a company, this website streamlines the process, ensuring smooth operations and optimal vehicle upkeep. Explore how this project brings convenience and efficiency to car maintenance and garage management needs.
